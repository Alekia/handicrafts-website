The Future of India is here

Handicraft is one of the most important sectors in the Indian economy employing more than seven million people. The country produces woodware, artmetal wares, handprinted textiles, embroidered goods, zari goods, imitation jewellery, sculptures, pottery, glassware, attars, agarbattis, etc.

Providing quality products

Encouraging the usage of eco-friendly products

The main objective is to promote, support, protect, maintain and increase the export of handicrafts. The other activities are knowledge dissemination, providing professional advice and support to members, encouraging for more usage of eco-friendly products and creating awareness among the people for the usage of many beautiful products made out of materials like textiles, moldable and rigid materials, paper, plant fibers,clay etc.
